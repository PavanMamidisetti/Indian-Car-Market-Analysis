🚗 Indian Car Market Analysis

Data Analysis Project Using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib & Google Colab

📌 Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing the Indian Car Market dataset using Python and data analysis techniques. The objective is to identify market trends, understand vehicle pricing patterns, compare automobile brands, analyze fuel preferences, and derive meaningful business insights from the dataset.

The analysis helps consumers, dealerships, and automobile businesses make data-driven decisions regarding pricing, brand positioning, and market demand.

📊 Dataset Information:

  • Dataset Name: Indian Car Market Dataset
  • Total Records: 1,276 Cars
  • Total Features: 141 Columns
  • Dataset Type: Structured Automotive Dataset
  • Domain: Automobile Industry

The dataset contains detailed information about car specifications, engine capacity, transmission type, fuel type, dimensions, safety features, and pricing information.

💡 Key Insights

  • Most listed vehicles belong to a few dominant automobile brands.
  • Petrol vehicles dominate the Indian automobile market.
  • Premium brands command significantly higher average prices.
  • Engine specifications influence vehicle pricing.
  • Several technical features show positive correlation with car price.
  • Market demand varies significantly across fuel and transmission types.
